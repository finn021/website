## Jackbox Party Pack 1
### You Don't Know Jack 2015
A fast-paced trivia game with quirky and irreverent humor that keeps players on their toes.  
2–4 players | Remote play possible.

### Fibbage XL
A hilarious bluffing game where players invent fake answers to obscure trivia questions, trying to fool their opponents.  
2–8 players | Remote play possible.

### Drawful
Players draw ridiculous and often challenging prompts for others to guess, resulting in lots of laughs and artistic missteps.  
3–8 players | Remote play possible.

### Word Spud
A lighthearted word association game where players build on each other's words to create unexpected and funny connections.  
2–8 players | Works better in person.

### Lie Swatter
Test your knowledge and instincts by determining whether outrageous statements are true or false in this party game for any size group.  
1–100 players | Remote play possible.

In a recent executive order titled "Defending Women from Gender Ideology Extremism and Restoring Biological Truth to the Federal Government," Trump has inadvertently defined every American as having no gender.

> (d) “Female” means a person belonging, **at conception**, to the sex that produces the large reproductive cell.
> (e) “Male” means a person belonging, **at conception**, to the sex that produces the small reproductive cell.

Whoever wrote this tried and failed extremely to define gender which is ironic. At conception, all human embryos are identical in terms of reproductive structures and do not yet have differentiated reproductive organs. It isn't until about six weeks into development that embryos start developing characteristics that will eventually lead to male or female organs. Who the fuck wrote this.

The executive order defines that there are only two genders, which cannot be changed. It mandates that all government agencies must only list "male" or "female" on forms. and IDs such as passports and driver's licenses must list an individual's biological sex and remove the designation of 'X'. It also instructs agencies to eliminate all resources and communications that "promote" gender ideology (which also includes many resources on LGB which are taken down as they include T, Q and I). This is just one of the many unnecessary measures included in the order.

It also completely ignores and invalidates intersex people, which are **born** with differing characteristics that don't fit definitions of either male or female and make up approx. 5.6 million people in the US.
